Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry is a professional Basketball player who plays in Golden States. He is the son of Dell Curry who use to be the NBA player as well and his brother Seth Curry is also a NBA player right now He went to Davidson College and he started to gain attention from people. He was drafted to Golden States in 2009 and he made huge contribution to make his team win the Championship Game in 2015. Also he was awarded Season MVP in 2016
